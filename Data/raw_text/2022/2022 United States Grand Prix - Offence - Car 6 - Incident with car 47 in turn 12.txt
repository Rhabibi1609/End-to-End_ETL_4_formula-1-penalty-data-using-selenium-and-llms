To The Team Manager, Date 23 October 2022
Williams Racing
Time 16:05
The Stewards, having received a report from the Race Director, have considered the following
matter and determine the following:
No / Driver 6 - Nicholas Latifi
Competitor Williams Racing
Time 15:15
Session Race
Fact Car 6 collided with car 47 at turn 12.
Offence Breach of Appendix L Ch IV 2d) of the FIA International Sporting Code.
Decision 5 second time penalty
2 penalty points (total of 5 for the 12-month period).
Reason The Stewards reviewed the video evidence and determined that car 6 collided with
car 47 at turn 12 forcing car 47 off the track.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the
Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and
Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
