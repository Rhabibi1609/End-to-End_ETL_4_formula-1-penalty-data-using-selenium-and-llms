The following drivers will use a new gearbox case and cassette (GBX C & C) for the
remainder of the Event:
Number Car Driver Previously used GBX C & C

The gearbox case and cassette used by Alexander Albon is one of the four new gearbox

The gearbox case and cassette used by Zhou Guanyu is the fifth of the four new gearbox

conformity with Article 29.2 c) of the 2022 Formula One Sporting Regulations.
