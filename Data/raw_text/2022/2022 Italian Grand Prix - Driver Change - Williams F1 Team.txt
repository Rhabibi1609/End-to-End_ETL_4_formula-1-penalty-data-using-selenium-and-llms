2022 ITALIAN GRAND PRIX
08 - 11 September 2022
From The Stewards Document 32
To The Team Manager, Date 10 September 2022
Williams Racing
Time 12:42
The Stewards have received a request from Williams F1 Team to change the driver of car 23,
Alexander Albon.
In accordance with Article 32.2 of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations, the Stewards grant
permission for the driver, Nyck de Vries, with the car number 45, to participate in the 2022 Italian
Grand Prix in place of Alexander Albon.
The driver of car 45 is required to use the engine, gearbox and tyres which were allocated to the
original driver, Alexander Albon as detailed under Article 32.4 of the FIA Formula One Sporting
Regulations. Further, this permission is subject to Nyck de Vries passing the scrutineering
requirements.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the Stewards, in
accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial
and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
Garry Connelly Loic Bacquelaine
Mika Salo Paolo Longoni
