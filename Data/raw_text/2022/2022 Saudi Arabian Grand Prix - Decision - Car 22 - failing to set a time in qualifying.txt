2022 SAUDI ARABIAN GRAND PRIX
24 - 27 March 2022
From The Stewards Document 41
To The Team Manager, Date 27 March 2022
Scuderia AlphaTauri
Time 00:20
The Stewards have received a request from Scuderia AlphaTauri to allow car 22 to start the race
despite failing to set a qualifying time in Q1.
In accordance with Article 42.2 of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations, the Stewards grant
permission for car 22, Yuki Tsunoda, to start the race, as the driver has set satisfactory times in
practice at this Event. The car will be placed on the grid in accordance with Article 42.2 of the FIA
Formula One Sporting Regulations.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the Stewards, in
accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and Article 10.1.1 of the FIA
Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
