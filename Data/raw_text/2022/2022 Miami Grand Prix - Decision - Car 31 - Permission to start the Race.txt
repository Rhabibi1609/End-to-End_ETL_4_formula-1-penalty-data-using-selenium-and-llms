2022 MIAMI GRAND PRIX
5 - 8 May 2022
From The Stewards Document 43
To The Team Manager, Date 07 May 2022
BWT Alpine F1 Team
Time 17:35
The Stewards have received a request from BWT Alpine F1 Team to allow car 31 to start the race
despite failing to set a qualifying time in Q1.
In accordance with Article 42.2 of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations, the Stewards grant
permission for car 31, Esteban Ocon, to start the race, as the driver has set satisfactory times in
practice at this Event. The car will be placed on the grid in accordance with Article 42.2 of the FIA
Formula One Sporting Regulations.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the Stewards, in
accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial
and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
