2022 AZERBAIJAN GRAND PRIX
9 - 12 June 2022
From The Stewards Document 42
To The Team Manager, Date 12 June 2022
Williams Racing
Time 15:19
The Stewards, having received a report from the Race Director, have considered the following
matter and determine the following:
No / Driver 6 - Nicholas Latifi
Competitor Williams Racing
Time 14:59
Session Race
Fact Personnel touched the car after the 15 second warning during the starting procedure
and the car consequently failed to start from the Pit Lane.
Offence Breach of Article 44.6 of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations.
Decision 10 Seconds Stop and Go.
Reason The Stewards reviewed the video evidence and determined that a member of the
team touched the car after the 15 second warning during the starting procedure.
There is a mandatory requirement under Article 44.6 for the car to start from the Pit
Lane in these circumstances and if the car fails to do so, a mandatory 10 Seconds
Stop and Go penalty is imposed.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the
Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and
Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
