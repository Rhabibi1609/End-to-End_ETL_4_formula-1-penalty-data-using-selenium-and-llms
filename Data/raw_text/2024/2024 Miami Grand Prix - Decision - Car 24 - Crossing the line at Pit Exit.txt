To The Team Manager, Date 04 May 2024
Stake F1 Team Kick Sauber
Time 20:56
The Stewards, having received a report from the Race Director, summoned (document 55) and
heard from the driver and team representative, have considered the following matter and determine
the following:
No / Driver 24 - Zhou Guanyu
Competitor Stake F1 Team Kick Sauber
Time 16:16
Session Qualifying
Fact Car 24 crossed the white line at Pit Exit.
InfringementBreach of Appendix L, Chapter IV Article 6 of the International Sporting Code and
non-compliance with Race Director’s Event Notes (item 11, document 36).
Decision Reprimand (Driving)
This is the driver’s 1st reprimand of the season.
Reason The Stewards reviewed video evidence and Car 24 crossed the line at pit exit.
The Stewards issued a Reprimand (driving) which is the standard penalty for this
infringement. We did not consider this infringement to be dangerous as it was right at
the end of the pit exit line and there was no other car nearby.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the
Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and
Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
Decisions of the Stewards are taken independently of the FIA and are based solely
on the relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
