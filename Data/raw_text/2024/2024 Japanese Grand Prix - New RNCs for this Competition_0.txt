Technical Delegate’s Report
The following drivers are using a new gearbox case and cassette (GBX C & C) for the
remainder of the Competition:
Number Car Driver Previously used GBX C & C
16 Ferrari Charles Leclerc 1
02 Williams Mercedes Logan Sargeant 1
77 Kick Sauber Ferrari Valtteri Bottas 1
27 Haas Ferrari Nico Hülkenberg 1
The gearbox case and cassette used by the above drivers is one (1) of the five (5) new gearbox
cases and cassettes allowed for the 2024 Championship season and this is in conformity with
Article 29.2 of the 2024 Formula One Sporting Regulations.
The following drivers are using a new gearbox driveline, gear change components and auxiliary
components (GBX DL) for the remainder of the Competition:
Number Car Driver Previously used GBX DL
16 Ferrari Charles Leclerc 1
02 Williams Mercedes Logan Sargeant 1
77 Kick Sauber Ferrari Valtteri Bottas 1
27 Haas Ferrari Nico Hülkenberg 1
The gearbox driveline, gear change components and auxiliary components used by the above
drivers is one (1) of the five (5) new gearbox drivelines, gear change components and auxiliary
components allowed for the 2024 Championship season and this is in conformity with Article
29.2 of the 2024 Formula One Sporting Regulations.
