2024 AUSTRIAN GRAND PRIX
28 - 30 June 2024
From The Stewards Document 5
To The Team Manager, Date 28 June 2024
Oracle Red Bull Racing
Time 08:30
The Stewards, having received a report from the Media Director, have considered the following
matter and determine the following:
No / Driver 1 - Max Verstappen
Competitor Oracle Red Bull Racing
Time 13:30
Fact Late attendance of the Thursday Press Conference.
InfringementBreach of Article 19.1 a) of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations.
Decision No penalty is applied.
Reason The Stewards received a report from the Media Delegate that the driver of Car 1
(Max Verstappen), was late for the Thursday Press Conference.
The Media Delegate reported that the driver had some transportation issues on the
way to the track, but the team informed the FIA in advance. The Stewards conclude
that the driver and the team took every reasonable measure given the circumstances
and therefore impose no penalty despite technically the regulations have been
breached.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the
Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and
Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
Decisions of the Stewards are taken independently of the FIA and are based solely
on the relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
