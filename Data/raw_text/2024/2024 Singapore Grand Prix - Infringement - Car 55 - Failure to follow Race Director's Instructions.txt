2024 SINGAPORE GRAND PRIX
20 - 22 September 2024
From The Stewards Document 34
To The Team Manager, Date 21 September 2024
Scuderia Ferrari
Time 23:39
The Stewards, having received a report from the Race Director, summoned (document 29) and
heard from the driver and team representative, have considered the following matter and determine
the following:
No / Driver 55 - Carlos Sainz
Competitor Scuderia Ferrari
Time 21:03
Session Qualifying
Fact Failure to follow the Race Director’s Event Notes.
InfringementAlleged breach of Article 12.2.1 i) of the International Sporting Code and
noncompliance with Race Director’s Event Note (item 17, document 3).
Decision Warning.
Reason The Stewards heard from the driver of Car 55 (Carlos Sainz), team representative
and reviewed, video, and in-car video evidence.
The driver failed to go around the bollard at Turn 3 having left the track at turn 2
during Q1. The driver explained that he left the track because he was out of control
and was concerned with regaining control and as it was qualifying know that his lap
time would be deleted.
Considering that the lap was deleted, which is a penalty and no advantage was gained
and no other driver impeded, the Stewards issue a warning.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the
Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and
Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
Decisions of the Stewards are taken independently of the FIA and are based solely
on the relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
