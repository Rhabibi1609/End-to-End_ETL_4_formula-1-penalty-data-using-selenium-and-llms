29 February - 02 March 2024
From The Stewards Document 11
To The Team Manager, Date 29 February 2024
Stake F1 Team Kick Sauber
Time 19:45
The Stewards, having received a report from the Race Director, summoned (document 9) and heard
from the driver and team representative, have considered the following matter and determine the
following:
No / Driver 77 - Valtteri Bottas
Competitor Stake F1 Team Kick Sauber
Time 17:59
Session Practice 2
Fact Crossed the Pit Exit line at 17:59:56 while the red light was on.
InfringementBreach of Article 37.2 of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations.
Decision Reprimand (Driving).
This is the driver’s 1st reprimand of the season.
Reason The Stewards heard from the driver of Car 7​​ 7 (Valtteri Bottas), team representative
and reviewed video evidence and determined that Car 77 crossed the Pit Exit Line
while the red light was on. The driver stopped shortly after crossing the line and
admitted his error at the hearing. We accordingly imposed a reprimand with no
penalty points.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the
Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and
Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
Decisions of the Stewards are taken independently of the FIA and are based solely
on the relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
