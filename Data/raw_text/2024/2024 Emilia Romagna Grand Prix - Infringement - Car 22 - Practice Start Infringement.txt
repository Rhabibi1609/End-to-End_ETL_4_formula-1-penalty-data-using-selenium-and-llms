The Stewards, having received a report from the Race Director, summoned (document 27) and
heard from the driver and team representative, have considered the following matter and determine
the following:
No / Driver 22 - Yuki Tsunoda
Competitor Visa Cash App RB F1 Team
Time 17:40
Session Practice 2
Fact Completed a practice start in the incorrect location.
InfringementBreach of Article 12.2.1 i) of the International Sporting Code and non-compliance with
Race Director’s Event Note (item 9.1, document 4).
Decision Reprimand (Driving).
This is the driver’s 1st reprimand of the season.
Reason The Stewards heard from the driver of Car 22 (Yuki Tsunoda), team representative
and reviewed video.
Car 22 performed a practice start in violation of the Race Director’s Event Notes,
which only permit a practice start before the pit exit lights during free practice, as the
location where the driver did his practice start is considered dangerous during the
session.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the
Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and
Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
Decisions of the Stewards are taken independently of the FIA and are based solely
on the relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
