TECHNICAL DELEGATE’S REPORT
The following driver is using a new internal combustion engine (ICE) for the remainder of the
Competition:
Number Car Driver Previously used ICE
11 Red Bull Racing Honda RBPT Sergio Perez 4
The internal combustion engine used by Sergio Perez is the fifth (5) of the four (4) new internal
combustion engines allowed for the 2024 Championship season and this is not in conformity
with Article 28.2 of the 2024 Formula One Sporting Regulations.
The following driver is using a new energy store (ES) for the remainder of the Competition:
Number Car Driver Previously used ES
11 Red Bull Racing Honda RBPT Sergio Perez 2
The energy store used by Sergio Perez is the third (3) of the two (2) new energy stores allowed
for the 2024 Championship season and this is not in conformity with Article 28.2 of the 2024
Formula One Sporting Regulations.
The following driver is using a new control electronics (CE) for the remainder of the Competition:
Number Car Driver Previously used CE
11 Red Bull Racing Honda RBPT Sergio Perez 2
The control electronics used by Sergio Perez is the third (3) the two (2) new control electronics
allowed for the 2024 Championship season and this is not in conformity with Article 28.2 of the
2024 Formula One Sporting Regulations.
The following driver is using a new exhaust system (EX) for the remainder of the Competition:
Number Car Driver Previously used EX
11 Red Bull Racing Honda RBPT Sergio Perez 5
The exhaust system used by Sergio Perez is one (1) of the eight (8) new exhaust systems
allowed for the 2024 Championship season and this is in conformity with Article 28.2 of the
2024 Formula One Sporting Regulations.
The PU elements have been replaced without the approval of the FIA technical delegate, this
not being in conformity with Article 40.3 of the 2024 Formula One Sporting Regulations.
Therefore car number 23 should now be required to start the race from the pit lane according to
Article 40.9 of the 2024 Formula One Sporting Regulations.
I am referring this matter to the stewards for their consideration.
