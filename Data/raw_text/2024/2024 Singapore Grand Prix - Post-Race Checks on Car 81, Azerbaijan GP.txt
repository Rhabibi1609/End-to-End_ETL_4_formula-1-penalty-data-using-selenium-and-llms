Technical Delegate’s Report
After the race in Baku, car number 81 was randomly chosen among the top ten cars for more
extensive physical inspections.
Subject to these physical inspections was the rear brake system. The following points were
examined:
TR Article 3.13.2 – Drums
TR Article 3.13.3 – Scoops
TR Article 3.13.4 – Internal cooling ducts
TR Article 3.13.5 – Front drum deflector
TR Article 3.13.6 – Rear drum deflector
TR Article 8.3 – Homologation status for all sensors and actuators relating to the rear brake system
TR Article 11.1 – Failure mode of the system and dimensional design constraints of the master
cylinder
TR Article 11.1 – Mechanisms that could provide asymmetric braking torques for a given axle
TR Article 11.4 – Pressure in the rear calipers does not increase above that achieved by the applied
force to the brake pedal
Accuracy of the schematics supplied as part of TD001 submission in comparison with the physical
system
All inspected components were found to be in conformance with the 2024 Formula One Technical
Regulations.
Jo Bauer
The FIA Formula One Technical Delegate
