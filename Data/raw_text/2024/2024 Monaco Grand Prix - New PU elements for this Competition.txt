Technical Delegate’s Report
The following drivers will start the eighth Competition of the 2024 Formula One World
Championship with a new energy store (ES):
Number Car Driver Previously used ES
01 Red Bull Racing Honda RBPT Max Verstappen 1
20 Haas Ferrari Kevin Magnussen 1
27 Haas Ferrari Nico Hülkenberg 1
The energy store used by the above drivers is one (1) of the two (2) new energy stores allowed
for the 2024 Championship season and this is in conformity with Article 28.2 of the 2024
Formula One Sporting Regulations.
