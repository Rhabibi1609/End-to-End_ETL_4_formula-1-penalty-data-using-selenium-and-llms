TECHNICAL DELEGATE’S REPORT
The following driver will use a new gearbox case and cassette (GBX C & C) for the
remainder of the Competition:
Number Car Driver Previously used GBX C & C
20 Haas Ferrari Kevin Magnussen 1
The gearbox case and cassette used by the above driver is one (1) of the five (5) new
gearbox cases and cassettes allowed for the 2024 Championship season and this is in
conformity with Article 29.2 of the 2024 Formula One Sporting Regulations.
The following driver will use a new gearbox driveline, gear change components and
auxiliary components (GBX DL) for the remainder of the Competition:
Number Car Driver Previously used GBX DL
20 Haas Ferrari Kevin Magnussen 1
The gearbox driveline, gear change components and auxiliary components used by the
above driver is one (1) of the five (5) new gearbox drivelines, gear change components
and auxiliary components allowed for the 2024 Championship season and this is in
conformity with Article 29.2 of the 2024 Formula One Sporting Regulations.
