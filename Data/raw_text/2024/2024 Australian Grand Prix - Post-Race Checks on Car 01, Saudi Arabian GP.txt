2024 AUSTRALIAN GRAND PRIX
22 - 24 March 2024
From The FIA Formula One Technical Delegate Document 2
To The Stewards Date 20 March 2024
Time 18:36
After the race of the Saudi Arabian GP, car number 01 was randomly chosen among the top ten cars
for more extensive physical inspections.
Subject to these physical inspections were:
- all front and rear suspension dampers
- the engine air intake system
- the oil and coolant system and charge air cooling
- all sensors related to the above systems
All inspected components were found to be in conformance with the 2024 Formula One Technical
Regulations.
Manuel Leal
The FIA Formula One Technical Delegate
