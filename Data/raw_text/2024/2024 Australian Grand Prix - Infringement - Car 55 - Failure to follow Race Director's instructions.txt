2024 AUSTRALIAN GRAND PRIX
22 - 24 March 2024
From The Stewards Document 28
To The Team Manager, Date 23 March 2024
Scuderia Ferrari
Time 14:44
The Stewards, having received a report from the Race Director, summoned (document 27) and
heard from the driver and team representative, have considered the following matter and determine
the following:
No / Driver 55 - Carlos Sainz
Competitor Scuderia Ferrari
Time 13:32
Session Practice 3
Fact Completed a practice start before taking the end of session signal.
InfringementBreach of Article 12.2.1 i) of the International Sporting Code, non-compliance with
Race Director’s Event Note (item 10, document 3).
Decision Reprimand (Driving).
Reason The Stewards heard from the driver of Car 5​​ 5 (Carlos Sainz), team representative
and reviewed positioning data, team radio and video.
Car 55 performed a practice start in violation of the Race Director’s Event Notes,
which only permit a practice start once the car has crossed the Line. Car 55 had not
crossed the line, and while the Stewards recognized that the situation ahead of him
was confusing, consistent with prior decisions, they issue a Reprimand (Driving).
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the
Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and
Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
Decisions of the Stewards are taken independently of the FIA and are based solely
on the relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
