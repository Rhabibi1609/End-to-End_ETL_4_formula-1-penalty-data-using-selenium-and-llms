hand tyres crossed the outside of the solid yellow line at pit exit in violation of the
code.
While the penalty guidelines indicate a penalty point, the Stewards elect not to apply a
penalty point because (a) during the driverâ€™s meeting the drivers indicated that this
line is difficult to see at this time of day, (b) his tyres crossed for less than a meter,
and (c) there was no danger caused.
