From The Stewards Document 40
To The Team Manager, Date 25 May 2024
MoneyGram Haas F1 Team
Time 20:26
The Stewards have received a request from MoneyGram Haas F1 Team to allow Car 20 to start the
Race despite failing to set a lap time in qualifying.
In accordance with Article 39.4 of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations, the Stewards grant
permission for Car 20, Kevin Magnussen, to start the Race, as the driver has set satisfactory times
in practice at this Event. The car will be placed on the grid in accordance with Article 42.1 of the FIA
Formula One Sporting Regulations.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the Stewards, in
accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial
and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
Decisions of the Stewards are taken independently of the FIA and are based solely on the relevant
regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
