From The FIA Formula One Technical Delegate Document 12
To The Stewards Date 03 November 2023
Time 11:33
Technical Delegate’s Report
Wednesday night team personnel of the McLaren Formula 1 Team, who are associated with the
operation of the car, were within the confines of the circuit during the thirteen hour period which
commenced at 17:30 on 01 November, fourtytwo hours before the scheduled start time of P1 and
ends twentynine hours before the scheduled start time of P1 at 06:30 on 02 November.
This was the first of the four individual exceptions permitted for the McLaren Formula 1 Team during
the 2023 Formula One Championship season and therefore no action should be taken.
Last night team personnel of the Alfa Romeo F1 Team Stake, who are associated with the operation
of the car, were within the confines of the circuit during the fourteen hour period which commenced
at 17:30 on 02 November, eighteen hours before the scheduled start time of P1 and ends four hours
before the scheduled start time of P1 at 07:30 on 03 November.
This was the first of the three individual exceptions permitted for the Alfa Romeo F1 Team Stake
during the 2023 Formula One Championship season and therefore no action should be taken.
Jo Bauer
The FIA Formula One Technical Delegate
