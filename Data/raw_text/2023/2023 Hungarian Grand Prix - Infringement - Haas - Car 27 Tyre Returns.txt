2023 HUNGARIAN GRAND PRIX
21 - 23 July 2023
From The Stewards Document 29
To The Team Manager, Date 22 July 2023
MoneyGram Haas F1 Team
Time 17:48
The Stewards, having received a report from the Technical Delegate (document 21), summoned
(document 23) and heard from the team representative, have considered the following matter and
determine the following
No / Driver 27 - Nico Hulkenberg
Competitor MoneyGram Haas F1 Team
Time 16:02
Session Qualifying
Fact The team failed to electronically return tyres before the start of the Qualifying
session.
InfringementBreach of Article 30.5 g) of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations.
Decision The competitor (MoneyGram Haas F1 Team) is fined €5,000.
Reason The Stewards heard from the team representative and determined that the team had
not electronically returned one set of intermediate tyres for Car 2​​ 7 (Nico
Hulkenberg), before the start of the qualifying session, although they had physically
returned the tyres to the tyre supplier. This is a technical breach of Article 30.5 (g) of
the Sporting Regulations.
Consistently with previous precedents for such an infringement, we fined the team
€5,000.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the
Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and
Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
Decisions of the Stewards are taken independently of the FIA and are based solely
on the relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
