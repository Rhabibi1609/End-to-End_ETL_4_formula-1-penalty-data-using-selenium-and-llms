Decision of the Stewards
In accordance with Article 11.9.3.o of the FIA International Sporting Code, having received a request

Official Programme of the 2023 Las Vegas Grand Prix by postponing the Drivers Meeting specified
in Article 20.2 of the Sporting Regulations, from 0230 hrs Friday to 1830 hrs Friday.
