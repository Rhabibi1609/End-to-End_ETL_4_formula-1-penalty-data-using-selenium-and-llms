2023 AUSTRALIAN GRAND PRIX
31 March - 02 April 2023
From The FIA Formula One Race Director Document 21
To All Teams, All Officials Date 31 March 2023
Time 20:20
Note to Teams
In order to ensure that cars are not driven unnecessarily slowly on in-laps during and after the end of
Qualifying or during reconnaissance laps when the pit exit is opened for the Race, drivers must stay
below 1:35.0 between the Safety Car lines shown on the pit lane map.
Niels Wittich
The FIA Formula One Race Director
