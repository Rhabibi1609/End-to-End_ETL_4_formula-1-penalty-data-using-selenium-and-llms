Decision of the Stewards
Schedule Clarification
In accordance with Article 11.9.1 of the FIA International Sporting Code, the Stewards make the
following determination:
In view of the unique schedule for this competition, for the purpose of reference to “days” in the
Formula 1 Sporting Regulations, Qualifying and the Race will be considered to occur on different
days as they would during a normal Formula 1 competition weekend.
