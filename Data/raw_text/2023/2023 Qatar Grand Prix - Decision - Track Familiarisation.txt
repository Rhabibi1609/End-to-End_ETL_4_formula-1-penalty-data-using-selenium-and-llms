2023 QATAR GRAND PRIX
06 - 08 October 2023
From The Stewards Document 30
To All Teams, All Officials Date 07 October 2023
Time 14:05
Decision of the Stewards
In accordance with Article 11.9.3.o of the FIA International Sporting Code, having received a request
from the Clerk of the Course and in the interests of safety, we hereby modify the Official Programme
of the 2023 Qatar Grand Prix by adding a Track Familiarisation session of 10 minutes starting at
16:00 hrs. The start of the Sprint Shootout will be moved to 16:20 hrs accordingly.
Notwithstanding Article 39.1 b) of the Formula One Sporting Regulations, the Sprint session will start
at 20:30 hrs.
Felix Holter Matteo Perini
Danny Sullivan David Domingo
The Stewards
