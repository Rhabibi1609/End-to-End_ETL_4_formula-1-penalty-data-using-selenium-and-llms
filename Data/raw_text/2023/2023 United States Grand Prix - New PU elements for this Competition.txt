Technical Delegate’s Report
The following driver will start the nineteenth Competition of the 2023 Formula One World
Championship with a new motor generator unit-kinetic (MGU-K):
Number Car Driver Previously used MGU-K
20 Haas Ferrari Kevin Magnussen 3
The motor generator unit-kinetic used by Kevin Magnussen is one of the four new motor
generator units-kinetic allowed for the 2023 Championship season and this is in conformity
with Article 28.2 of the 2023 Formula One Sporting Regulations.
The following drivers will start the nineteenth Competition of the 2023 Formula One World
Championship with a new exhaust system (EX):
Number Car Driver Previously used EX
27 Haas Ferrari Nico Hülkenberg 6
03 AlphaTauri RBPT Daniel Ricciardo 5
The exhaust system used by the above drivers is one of the eight new exhaust systems
allowed for the 2023 Championship season and this is in conformity with Article 28.2 of the
2023 Formula One Sporting Regulations.
