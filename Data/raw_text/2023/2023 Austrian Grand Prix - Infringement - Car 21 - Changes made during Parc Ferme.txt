2023 AUSTRIAN GRAND PRIX
30 June - 02 July 2023
From The Stewards Document 55
To The Team Manager, Date 02 July 2023
Scuderia AlphaTauri
Time 13:04
The Stewards, having received a report from the TechnicalDelegate (document 51),
have considered the following matter and determine the following:
No / Driver 21 - Nyck de Vries
Competitor Scuderia AlphaTauri
Time 12:00
Session Qualifying
Fact The following Power Unit elements have been used:
3rd Energy Store (ES)
3rd Control Electronics (CE)
All Power Unit elements were replaced without the approval of the FIA Technical
Delegate during Parc Ferme.
The suspension setup has been changed while under Parc Fermé.
The rear wing and beam wing have been changed to a different specification than that
which was used during qualifying
InfringementBreach of Articles 28.2 and 40.9 of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations.
Decision Required to start the Race from the pit lane.
Reason The Power Unit elements have been changed.
The setup was modified whilst the car was in parc fermé without the approval of the
Technical Delegate.
The wing setup has been changed from that previously used in Qualifying. The
competitor is therefore required to start the race from the pit lane per article 40.9 of
the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the
Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and
Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
Decisions of the Stewards are taken independently of the FIA and are based solely
on the relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
