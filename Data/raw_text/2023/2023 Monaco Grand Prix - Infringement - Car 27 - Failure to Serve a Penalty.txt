2023 MONACO GRAND PRIX
26 - 28 May 2023
From The Stewards Document 52
To The Team Manager, Date 28 May 2023
MoneyGram Haas F1 Team
Time 16:45
The Stewards, having received a report from the Race Director, have considered the following
matter and determine the following:
No / Driver 27 - Nico Hulkenberg
Competitor MoneyGram Haas F1 Team
Time 16:16
Session Race
Fact Failure to serve a penalty during a pit stop.
Infringment Breach of Article 54.4 (c) of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations
Decision 10 second time penalty
Reason Car 27 entered the pits having received a 5 second penalty previously. The pit stop
was observed on multiple angles of video by the FIA Race Operations Centre and
subsequently was checked by the Stewards and the team failed to serve the full 5
seconds penalty before working on the car.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the
Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and
Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
