2023 DUTCH GRAND PRIX
25 - 27 August 2023
From The Stewards Document 25
To The Team Manager, Date 25 August 2023
Scuderia AlphaTauri
Time 20:16
The Stewards have received a request from Scuderia AlphaTauri to change the driver of car 3,
Daniel Ricciardo.
In accordance with Article 32.2 of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations, the Stewards grant
permission for the driver, Liam Lawson, with the car number 40, to participate in the 2023 Dutch
Grand Prix in place of Daniel Ricciardo.
The driver of car 40 is required to use the engine, gearbox and tyres which were allocated to the
original driver, Daniel Ricciardo as detailed under Article 32.5 of the FIA Formula One Sporting
Regulations. Further, this permission is subject to Liam Lawson passing the scrutineering
requirements.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the Stewards, in
accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial
and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
Decisions of the Stewards are taken independently of the FIA and are based solely on the relevant
regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
