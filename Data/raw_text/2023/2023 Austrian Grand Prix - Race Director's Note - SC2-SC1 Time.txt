2023 AUSTRIAN GRAND PRIX
30 June - 02 July 2023
From The FIA Formula One Race Director Document 20
To All Teams, All Officials Date 30 June 2023
Time 15:45
Note to Teams
In order to ensure that cars are not driven unnecessarily slowly on in-laps during and after the end of
Qualifying, the Sprint Shootout or during reconnaissance laps when the pit exit is opened for the
Sprint and Race, drivers must stay below 1:10.0 between the Safety Car lines shown on the pit lane
map.
Niels Wittich
The FIA Formula One Race Director
