Technical Delegate’s Report
After the race of the British GP, car number 01 was randomly chosen among the top ten cars for
more extensive physical inspections.
Subject to these physical inspections was the fuel system according to TR Articles 5.11 and 6 as
well as the team’s submission relating the fuel system according to TD001 F 3.1.
All inspected components were found to be in conformance with the 2023 Formula One Technical
Regulations.
Jo Bauer
The FIA Formula One Technical Delegate
