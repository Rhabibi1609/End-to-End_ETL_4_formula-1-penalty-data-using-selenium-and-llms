2023 AUSTRIAN GRAND PRIX
30 June - 02 July 2023
From The Stewards Document 54
To The Team Manager, Date 02 July 2023
MoneyGram Haas F1 Team
Time 12:58
The Stewards, having received a report from the Race Director, have considered the following
matter and determine the following:
No / Driver 20 - Kevin Magnussen
Competitor MoneyGram Haas F1 Team
Time 12:00
Session Qualifying
Fact The suspension setup has been changed while under Parc Fermé.
InfringementBreach of Article 40.9 of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations.
Decision Required to start the Race from the pit lane.
Reason The setup was modified whilst the car was in parc fermé with the approval of the
Technical Delegate further to a written request.
The suspension setup has been changed from that previously used in Qualifying. The
competitor is therefore required to start the race from the pit lane per article 40.9 of
the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations.
Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the
Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and
Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
Decisions of the Stewards are taken independently of the FIA and are based solely
on the relevant regulations, guidelines and evidence presented.
